What is TimeMap

TimeMap is a calendar that makes your scheduling easy and simple.
It's also a personal assitant that reminds you of your upcoming events and the time to leave for your next event.

Never be late again

TimeMap notifies you when it's time to leave for your next event, according to your current location and your way of traveling. Never be late again!

Label your events

Family, school, work, life is complicated and things gets mixed up. Label your events with different tags and priorities, make your life simple and organized once again.

Get ready for a new day

The best way to get you ready for the upcoming challenges. A breif reminder gives you a quick summary of your next day right before sleep.
